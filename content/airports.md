# Nearby Airports

## Best Airport for Most Guests
### Milan Malpensa (MXP)
- **Status:** Best choice for easiest train connections.
- **Typical route:** Malpensa Express → Saronno → Como Lago / Como S. Giovanni.
- **Typical total cost:** ~€11–15.

## Other Airport Options
### Milan Linate (LIN)
- **Best for:** Flights with convenient Milan connections.
- **Typical route:** Shuttle/metro to Milano Centrale → Train to Como S. Giovanni.
- **Typical total cost:** ~€15–20.

### Milan Bergamo (BGY)
- **Best for:** Low-cost airlines.
- **Typical route:** Bus to Milano Centrale → Train to Como.
- **Typical total cost:** ~€15–25.

### Lugano Airport (LUG)
- **Best for:** Swiss connections and short travel time.
- **Typical route:** Regional train to Como S. Giovanni.
- **Typical total cost:** ~€10–15.

## Money + Stress Savers
- Buy train tickets in advance when possible.
- Keep screenshots of tickets in case of poor signal.
- If arriving late at night, check last train/bus before you fly.
