# Parking

For guests arriving with their own transportation, here are a few **paid parking areas in Como** where you can leave your car and then **walk or take a bus** to reach **Como Yacht Club**.

**Note:** Unfortunately, there is no free parking in the area.

## Paid Parking Options

### Parcheggio Lungolago
- **Walk:** 2 min walk
- **Note:** Easiest option. Walk directly to the venue.

### Centro Lago
- **Walk:** 7 min walk
- **Note:** Short walk along the lake. Or Bus N1 from Cavour.

### Como Central
- **Walk:** 15 min walk
- **Bus:** Bus N1 from *S. Rocchetto* to *Villa Olmo*.

### Autosilo Viale Lecco
- **Walk:** 20 min walk
- **Bus:** Bus N1 from *Cavour* to *Villa Olmo*.

## Bus Details (Linea N1)

### To Como Yacht Club
1. From **Como – S. Rocchetto** or **Cavour**, take **ASF Autolinee Linea N1** toward the lakefront.
2. Get off at **Como – Villa Olmo**, then walk a few minutes along the lake to **Como Yacht Club**.

### Saturday Night (Last Bus)
- Reference: **23:41** from **Como – Villa Olmo**.

### Important Note
Bus services can change. Re-check **Linea N1** on the official [ASF Autolinee](https://www.asfautolinee.it/search-for-lines-and-schedules/?lang=en) site.
