# Parking

There is **no free parking** close to Yacht Club Como. Plan for paid parking.

## Fastest Parking Choice
### Parcheggio Lungolago
- **Walk:** ~2 min
- **Why choose it:** Simplest option if available.

## Other Paid Options
### Centro Lago
- **Walk:** ~7 min
- **Alternative:** Bus N1 from Cavour.

### Como Central
- **Walk:** ~15 min
- **Bus option:** Bus N1 from S. Rocchetto to Villa Olmo.

### Autosilo Viale Lecco
- **Walk:** ~20 min
- **Bus option:** Bus N1 from Cavour to Villa Olmo.

## Public Transport Backup
- **Bus line:** ASF Autolinee N1.
- **Closest stop to venue:** Villa Olmo.
- **Saturday late bus reference:** around **23:41** from Villa Olmo (always re-check).
